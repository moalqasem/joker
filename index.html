WHAC-A-CLOWN

Webcam Hand-Tracking Arcade Game

HOW TO PLAY

  • 🖐️ Allow webcam access. Stand back so your upper body/hands are visible.
  • 🤡 Smack the clowns popping out of the holes with your hands.
  • ⚡ Hit power-ups: Freeze (blue) slows down time, Double Points (gold) doubles scores.
  • 💣 Avoid hitting the Bomb Clowns—they damage your health!
  • 📈 Clowns get faster and spawn more frequently as the timer runs down.
